How to Play:

  • Click/Tap on the buttons below: "Strike", "Throw", or "Block"
  • Fight logic:
    • Strikes beat Throws
    • Throws beat Blocks
    • Blocks beat Strikes
  • If one action beats the other, the losing player loses one Health Point!
  • If You AND Bear use the same attack (Strike or Throw) at the same time, you both lose one Health Point!
  • If You AND Bear block at the same time, no one uses a Health Point
  • Match goes on until You and/or Bear loses all HP
    • Double KOs are entirely possible!
